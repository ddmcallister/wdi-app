About Destanie

I am a project manager who took a coding class a year ago, hoping for a change of pace, and found something I enjoy so much it made me want to change careers. My current position has taught me a lot about the development process, and has helped me transition from being a slightly anti-social bookworm to a skilled communicator who is able to bridge the gap between lawyer-speak and tech-speak. However, I have always wanted to find work that would engage both my creative and my analytic sides. I am confident that working in web development will allow me to do this. Of course, I may never leave the bookworm fully behind—when I'm not coding, I still like to turn off all the screens and pick up a book.
